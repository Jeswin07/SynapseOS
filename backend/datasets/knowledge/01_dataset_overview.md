# Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset

## Overview

The Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset contains transactional information from one of Brazil's largest online marketplace platforms.

The data spans approximately 2016–2018 and includes nearly 100,000 customer orders collected across multiple marketplaces.

The dataset represents the complete customer purchase lifecycle, including:

- Customer information
- Orders
- Products
- Sellers
- Payments
- Reviews
- Logistics
- Delivery performance
- Geographic information

This dataset is widely used for:

- Business Intelligence
- Sales Analytics
- Customer Analytics
- Demand Forecasting
- Recommendation Systems
- Fraud Detection
- Supply Chain Optimization
- Machine Learning
- Enterprise AI Assistants
